Click the button to cycle through the chromatic scale (C → C# → D → ... → B → OFF). Each note has its own custom color: Peach, Terracotta, Apricot, Golden Hour, Aqua Dream, Seafoam, Teal, Lagoon, Dusty Rose, Sage, Lavender Haze, and Warm Cream.
