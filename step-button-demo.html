Unified StepButton Component Demo

Testing both Sequencer and Character Orchestra modes with a single component

Sequencer Mode (Hue-Based)

Square buttons with color intensity and saturation feedback. Click to toggle steps and cycle notes.

Features:
  • Click to toggle on/off and cycle through chromatic notes
  • Saturation increases with interaction level (press count)
  • Lightness indicates activity level
  • Active steps have a subtle glow effect
